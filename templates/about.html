Our Journey & Heritage

Ihsan Ul Haq & Sons General Supermarket is more than just a grocery store—it’s the story of dedication, resilience, and family values.

Our journey began in 1985, when our respected father, Mr. Ihsan Ul Haq, started a small grocery shop. In the early years, the business operated in partnership and faced many challenges. Through honesty and an unwavering commitment to serving the community, he continued moving forward despite every obstacle.

In 1990, he took a bold step and established his own independent grocery store. What started as a small neighborhood shop has grown into a trusted, well-established supermarket, proudly serving customers with quality products and exceptional service under one roof.
